EXPLORING ANIMAL LIFE

  1. CAT

    2. Cats win over their human friends with cute actions. They purr softly and knead with their paws when they're happy. They gently bump their heads against us to show they like us. When they play, chasing toys and jumping around, it's fun to watch. Sometimes they lick us like they groom themselves, which shows they care. Whether they're snuggling up or exploring curiously, cats bring joy to our lives with their expressive faces. These simple acts help cats create strong bonds with the people who love them.

  2. MONKEY

    3. Monkeys are smart and lively animals that live in forests and other warm places around the world. They enjoy playing and being together in groups called troops. Monkeys have clever hands and feet that help them grab things and swing from tree to tree. They eat all sorts of food like fruits, leaves, bugs, and sometimes small animals. There are different kinds of monkeys, such as capuchins and howlers, each adapted to where they live. Monkeys also talk to each other using different sounds and movements.
